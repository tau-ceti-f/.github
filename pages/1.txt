In the beginning

In the beginning, there was nothing. Nothing might seem like an easy concept but when you really try to comprehend it it makes no sense whatsoever. How can there be nothing? There has to be something right? Upon closer inspection you start to make out figures or shapes. Don't be fooled as this is just your brain trying to fill the void. There really is nothing. You know what that means right? It means all we can do is wait...
