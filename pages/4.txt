Nightcap

You always thought the name the scientists gave the concoction of pills was funny, a nightcap. As if taking them would clear your head and make you sleep better. Unfortunately, the contrary was true. The sleeping pills give you wild nightmares and hallucinations. The doctors assured you that it might be a possible side effect but that it is nothing to worry about. It indicates your brain responding normally to the anxiolytics and would make it easier to wake up when the time comes. One year is a hell of a long time to sleep but staying awake for the whole 12+ years in the vessel doesn't sound like an option worth considering either. You take the pills one by one. Starting with the vitamins and ending with the pink one. Any moment now the drowsiness could settle in...
